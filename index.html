About Me

Software Engineer

My name is Calvin Hart and I am a combination of a lot of things that make me a good Web Developer. When I was a kid I was always interested in how things worked. So much in fact that I joined an elite group of people who took apart their mothers appliances only to forget how it all went back together. Good times. As time went on, I couldn’t avoid the impact the internet had on our society, and naturally I couldn’t avoid my curiosity into wondering how all of these different coding languages and CMS platforms worked together to make a page where people can interact with the world and vice versa. So naturally, like my mothers walkman, I started picking it apart and getting a better look. As the recent pandemic changed the way we live, the same old questions started coming up for me “how is this going to work?”, and “how can I be involved?”. Realizing “technology leads, and society follows”, I believe being a good web developer will allow me to help my fellow person in maintaining their business in the age of social distancing, or just by staying connected to the world when it doesn’t seem possible. If you are in need of helping hand to adapt a strategy to keep evolving with all of the changes in our world, lets connect!
